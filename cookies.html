1. Introduction

QVXX Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (qvxx.ai). This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

6. Your Cookie Choices

6.1 Browser Settings

  • Delete cookies individually or entirely

  • Block all cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Please note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience on our website and limit the services we can offer. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are necessary for the website to function.

8. Do Not Track
